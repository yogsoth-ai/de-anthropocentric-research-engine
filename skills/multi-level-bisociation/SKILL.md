## Execution Guidance

1. **Decompose**: Use abstraction-ladder to place each concept on multiple levels (concrete → functional → structural → abstract)
2. **Identify Collision Points**: At each level, find where the two concepts' logics intersect or contradict
3. **Force Multi-Level Collision**: Simultaneously collide at 3+ levels
4. **Map Cross-Level Effects**: Identify how collision at one level creates opportunities at another
5. **Extract Insights**: Derive novel mechanisms from the multi-level collision pattern
6. **Validate Depth**: Confirm insights are structural, not surface-level puns
