## Execution Guidance

1. **Identify contradiction**: Find the core tension in the problem
2. **Compress**: Reduce to a 2-3 word oxymoron (e.g., "familiar surprise")
3. **Interpret**: Explore what the oxymoron suggests as solution directions
4. **Deepen**: Chain the symbolic image through analogy layers
5. **Extract**: Convert symbolic insights into concrete mechanisms
6. **Validate**: Check that solutions actually address the original contradiction
