## Execution Guidance

1. **Near future (5yr)**: What's the obvious trajectory? What incremental improvements matter?
2. **Medium future (50yr)**: What paradigm shifts are possible? What's obsolete?
3. **Far future (500yr)**: What's the problem even about at civilizational scale?
4. **Backcast**: From each future, what must be true NOW for that future to arrive?
5. **Extract**: What insights from backcasting suggest novel present-day actions?
