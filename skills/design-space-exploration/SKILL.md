## Execution Guidance

1. **Define Parameters**: Identify independent design dimensions
2. **Set Constraints**: Define hard constraints (must satisfy) and soft constraints (prefer)
3. **Enumerate**: Use combination-mapping to systematically generate configurations
4. **Filter**: Apply constraint satisfaction to eliminate infeasible combinations
5. **Explore White Space**: Focus on unexplored regions of the feasible space
6. **Evaluate Emergence**: Check novel configurations for emergent properties
7. **Synthesize**: Compile viable novel configurations into structured proposals
