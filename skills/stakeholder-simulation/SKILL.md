## Execution Guidance

1. **End User**: What do they actually need? What frustrates them? What delights them?
2. **Engineer**: Can this be built? What are the technical constraints and opportunities?
3. **Investor**: What's the ROI? What's the market? What's the competitive moat?
4. **Regulator**: What rules apply? What risks must be mitigated? What compliance is needed?
5. **Society**: What are second-order effects? Who benefits, who's harmed? What's ethical?
6. **Synthesize**: Where do stakeholder interests align or conflict? What solutions satisfy multiple stakeholders?
